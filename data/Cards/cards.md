# Legends of Noblesse - Cards & Assets

---

## Classes

### The Arch-Hierarch - Class

**XP: "Divine Insight"**  
Gain 1 XP every time you successfully activate a Non-Unit card during the Siege Phase.

**Level 1: Efficient Tithe**  
Once per turn during your Draw Phase, you may convert 2 Rations into 1 Special Resource of your choice.

**Level 3: Holy Aegis**  
During the Siege Phase, whenever you activate a Non-Unit card, you may choose one of your Units. it gains +1 Will until the end fo the Phase.

**Level 6: Miracle of Faith**  
Once per game, during the Preparations Phase, you may pay 1 Sacrifice and 1 Faith. Return a Felled Unit from your Grave directly to one of your Battalions face-down.

**Look:** Ceremonial high priest in layered robes and halo sigils, cathedral iconography and faith-gold trim.

---

### The Ash Chancellor - Class

**XP: "Ledger of Cinders"**  
Gain 1 XP whenever one or more of your Units are Felled in combat.

**Level 1: Cinder Tithe**  
Once per Siege Phase, when your Units are Felled, gain 1 Sacrifice.

**Level 3: Ashen Recall**  
Once per Preparations Phase, pay 1 Sacrifice to choose a Unit from your Grave and return it to your hand.

**Level 6: Pyre Decree**  
Once per Siege Phase, pay 2 Sacrifice to give your Front Line Units +2 Might until end of phase.

**Look:** Political war-mage wrapped in ash-dark vestments, ember seals and funeral smoke motifs.

---

### The Grand Strategist - Class

**XP: "Hold The Line"**  
Gain 1 XP every time a Battlefield remains as Neutral Ground or under Your active Control.

**Level 1: Tactical Bluff**  
Once per turn during the Preparations Phase, you may shuffle a card from your hand face-down into one of your unrevealed Battalions, then return a different face-down card from that Battalion to your hand.

**Level 3: Calculated Deployment**  
During the Siege Phase, if you lose the dice roll to determine who deploys first, you may pay 1 Magium to force a re-roll.

**Level 6: Tactical Gambit**  
Once per game, during the Siege Phase, after all Battalions have been deployed, but they are revealed, you may instantly swap the locations of your two Battalions.

**Look:** Veteran tactician over a holographic war map, measured posture, brass instruments, and command markers.

---

### The Storm Warden - Class

**XP: "Tempest Pressure"**  
Gain 1 XP each time your Battalion's damage overflows into an opposing Back Line during the Siege Phase.

**Level 1: Stormline Drill**  
During Siege, your Front Line Units gain +1 Might.

**Level 3: Static Surge**  
Once per Siege Phase, when you cause overflow, gain 1 Magium.

**Level 6: Eye of the Storm**  
Once per Siege Phase, pay 1 Magium to give one of your Front Line Units +2 Might until end of phase.

**Look:** Armored sentinel channeling storm arcs, wind-torn cloak, lightning veins in sky-tone frame accents.

---

### The Vanguard - Class

**XP: "Shatter The Ranks"**  
Gain 1 XP every time your Battalion’s damage successfully overflows into an opposing Back Line during the Siege Phase.

**Level 1: Marching Orders**  
Siege passive: Your Front Line units gain +1 Might while attacking a Battlefield you do not control.

**Level 3: Relentless Push**  
Once per Siege phase, you may pay 1 Ore or 1 Materia. Choose one of your Front Line Units; it gains +2 Might until the end of the phase.

**Level 6: Total Conquest**  
Once per game, when you successfully claim a Battlefield, you may immediately force your opponent to Fell one of their Units that survived on a different Battlefield before it can return to their Barracks.

**Look:** Spearhead commander charging from front lines, battle pennants and momentum-focused diagonal composition.
